Thanks for using OffLens. We’re here to help. Contact: shungyy8@gmail.com

How it works

  • All OCR runs on-device with VisionKit; no uploads, no tracking.
  • Pick or snap a photo, draw the crop box, then review highlights and copy text.
  • History (up to 100 entries) stays local; you can edit or delete anytime.

Common issues

  • Low accuracy? Try tighter crop, better lighting, and higher contrast.
  • Mixed languages? Enable auto-detect or select target languages in settings.
  • No results? Re-crop and retry; ensure the text is sharp and unobstructed.

Privacy Policy

1) On-device only. OffLens processes images and recognized text entirely on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos, crops, or OCR results to any server or third party.

2) No tracking or ads. The app includes no analytics SDKs, ad networks, or trackers. We do not collect identifiers, usage profiles, or telemetry.

3) Permissions. Camera and Photo Library permissions are requested only to capture or pick images and to optionally save cropped images. Declining limits those features but does not change offline OCR for images you already have.

4) Local history. OCR history (up to 100 entries) is stored locally on your device. You can edit or delete entries anytime; deletion removes them from your device.

5) Support contact. If you email shungyy8@gmail.com, your message is used solely to respond. We do not add you to mailing lists or share your email.

6) Children. OffLens is not directed to children under 13. Do not submit data if you are under the minimum age required by local law.

7) Security. Because processing is on-device and there is no backend storage, exposure is minimized. Protect your device with a passcode and keep your OS up to date.

8) Updates. We may update this policy in the app. Continued use after updates indicates acceptance of the latest version.
